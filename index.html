About

Graduated Magna Cum Laude in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering, my journey into technology started when I was awarded a scholarship from the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) in 2020. I initially dreamed of becoming a flight attendant to travel the world, but the scholarship required a path in science and technology. This led me to the exciting world of computers and websites.

Though I haven't traveled much, I found adventure online. Every website and app I visited felt like a new destination, sparking my passion for creating beautiful and simple user interfaces. Through my degree, I learned various programming languages and became fascinated by how websites work. This curiosity drove me to dive deeper into coding through self-study, code camps, online courses, and YouTube tutorials.

Now, I'm dedicated to mastering full stack development with CSS, JavaScript, Next.Js, React.Js, Python, SQL, and noSQL databases. My goal is to help businesses grow by building visually appealing and user-friendly websites. When I’m not coding, you'll find me listening to music, spending time with my loved ones, and hitting the gym to keep my brain sharp (or at least prevent it from turning to mush!).
